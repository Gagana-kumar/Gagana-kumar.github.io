Univ Arcade

Why?

Explain why do you want to pursue this Business Idea.

1. Accessibility and Affordability:

Why are you creating the app? To make sports grounds and arenas more accessible to the community.

2. Promoting Physical Activity:

Why is this important? Encouraging physical activity is crucial for overall health and well-being. By providing affordable access to sports facilities, you contribute to a healthier community.

3. Community Engagement:

Why involve the community? Building a sense of community around sports activities fosters social connections and a shared passion for fitness and recreation.

What?

What (Product/Service):

1. Mobile App for Slot Bookings:

What is the main offering? A mobile application that facilitates easy booking of slots for sports grounds and arenas.

2. Univ Arcade:

What is the significance of the name? "Univ Arcade" suggests a universal or inclusive space for sports and recreation. "Arcade" implies a diverse range of options, reflecting the variety of sports available.

3. Affordable Pricing Model:

What sets you apart? Providing an affordable pricing model makes sports participation accessible to a wider audience compared to traditional, potentially expensive alternatives.

How?

1. User-Friendly Interface:

How will the app work? The app will have an intuitive, user-friendly interface for easy navigation and quick slot bookings.

2. Integration with Sports Facilities:

How will you connect users with facilities? Establish partnerships with various sports facilities to integrate their availability into the app, allowing users to browse, select, and book slots seamlessly.

3. Secure Payment System:

How will payments be handled? Implement a secure and convenient payment system within the app to ensure a smooth booking process.
